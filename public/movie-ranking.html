My top 3 movies of all time.

Avengers: Endgame

"Avengers: Endgame" is a great movie because it masterfully brings together over a decade of storytelling in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, delivering a satisfying and emotionally charged conclusion to beloved character arcs.

Avengers: Infinity War

"Avengers: Infinity War" is remarkable because it balances an ensemble cast of beloved characters while delivering a complex, engaging narrative.

Deadpool & Wolverine

"Deadpool & Wolverine" brings together two iconic anti-heroes in the Marvel universe. The film brilliantly blends Deadpool’s humor and action with Wolverine’s gruff attitude and intense combat skills.
